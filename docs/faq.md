# FAQ

## Is this medical advice?

No. It is educational and documentation support.

## Can this skill prescribe Unani medicines?

No. It can create discussion templates and monograph structures, but doses and treatment decisions must come from qualified practitioners and authoritative sources.

## Can students use this for viva preparation?

Yes. It is well suited for topic outlines, concept maps, question banks, and model answers. Students should still verify with their teachers and official curriculum.

## Can clinics use this for OPD notes?

Yes, with practitioner review and de-identified data. The generated note should be treated as a draft.

## Does this replace textbooks?

No. Verify with prescribed textbooks, current curriculum materials, pharmacopeias, protocols, and teachers.

## Can I publish a case generated with this project?

Only if it is synthetic or properly de-identified and you have followed your institution’s ethics, consent, and publication policies.

## Can I add local language files?

Yes. Keep language simple, include warning signs, avoid prescriptions, and add clinician-review reminders.

# Medical and legal disclaimer

This repository is an educational and documentation-support project. It is not a medical device, not a diagnostic system, not a prescribing system, and not a replacement for a qualified registered practitioner.

## No clinical decision automation

Outputs from this project must be treated as drafts or checklists only. A qualified clinician or supervisor must review, verify, and approve any material before it is used in patient care, teaching, publication, or records.

## No emergency care replacement

If a patient may have a serious or emergency condition, seek urgent in-person medical assessment according to local emergency pathways. This project must not be used to delay urgent care.

## No prescribing

This project must not be used to generate final patient-specific prescriptions, doses, medicine changes, procedure instructions, or claims of safety. Drug monographs and treatment discussions must include verification fields and must be checked against authoritative sources and patient-specific contraindications.

## Evidence and traditional knowledge

Unani concepts may be documented for educational and clinical-history purposes. Any claim about efficacy, safety, indication, contraindication, dose, pharmacology, or modern biomedical correlation requires verification. Do not fabricate references, page numbers, trials, or classical citations.

## Local law and regulation

Users are responsible for complying with all applicable laws, medical council rules, institutional protocols, privacy requirements, and professional standards in their jurisdiction.

## Privacy

Do not upload, commit, publish, or share identifiable patient information. De-identify cases before using examples, issues, pull requests, or public discussions.

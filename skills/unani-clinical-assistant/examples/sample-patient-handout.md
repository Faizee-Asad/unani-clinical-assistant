# Sample Patient Handout

## Constipation: simple self-monitoring guide

This handout is educational and should be reviewed with your treating practitioner.

### What to notice

Track how often you pass stool, whether it is hard, whether there is pain, and whether diet, sleep, stress, or activity affects symptoms.

### Lifestyle points to discuss

- Regular meals
- Adequate fluids if not restricted by your doctor
- Gradual increase in physical activity if suitable
- Sleep routine
- Avoid ignoring the urge to pass stool

### Seek urgent care if

- Blood in stool or black stool
- Severe abdominal pain
- Vomiting with swelling of abdomen
- Unexplained weight loss
- Fever or severe weakness

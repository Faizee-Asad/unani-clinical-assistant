# Sample Viva Prep Output

## Topic: Zeequn Nafas / breathlessness topic discussion

### One-minute overview

Zeequn Nafas is discussed in Unani teaching as a breathing difficulty presentation. In viva, describe the Unani concept, key symptoms, triggering factors, red flags, and modern correlation with conditions such as asthma or other respiratory/cardiac causes, while avoiding over-specific diagnosis without examination.

### Likely viva questions

| Question | Model answer |
|---|---|
| What is the main complaint? | Difficulty in breathing or shortness of breath, described with duration, triggers, severity, and associated symptoms. |
| What red flags will you ask? | Severe breathlessness, chest pain, bluish lips, confusion, coughing blood, high fever, or low oxygen if measured. |
| Why modern correlation matters? | Breathlessness can arise from respiratory, cardiac, metabolic, anxiety-related, or emergency causes, so clinical assessment is essential. |

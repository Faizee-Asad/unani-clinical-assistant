# Sample Case-Taking Output

## User request

Create a case sheet for an adult patient with chronic constipation, poor sleep, low activity, and bloating. Do not prescribe.

## Skill-style output excerpt

### Safety note

Educational/documentation support only. A qualified practitioner should assess the patient and decide diagnosis/treatment.

### Red-flag screen

Ask urgently about:

- Blood in stool or black stool
- Severe abdominal pain or vomiting
- Unexplained weight loss
- New constipation in older age
- Fever, severe distension, or inability to pass stool/gas

If present, refer for urgent medical assessment.

### Missing information

- Duration and stool frequency
- Pain, bloating, vomiting
- Weight loss or bleeding
- Current medicines
- Diet, water intake, activity
- Past surgery or thyroid/diabetes history

### Unani documentation frame

Document Asbab-e-Sitta Zarooriya factors: food/drink, movement/rest, sleep/wakefulness, retention/evacuation, emotional state, and environment. Mizaj assessment requires trained evaluation.

# Sample Referral Letter

## Reason for referral

Adult patient with breathlessness and chest discomfort requires urgent assessment to rule out serious cardiopulmonary causes.

## Brief history

- Chief complaint: Breathlessness with chest discomfort
- Duration: Not provided
- Associated symptoms: Needs assessment for sweating, radiation of pain, fever, cough, wheeze, syncope
- Current medicines/allergies: Not provided

## Concern

Chest discomfort with breathlessness is a red-flag presentation and needs urgent qualified assessment.

## Request

Kindly assess and manage as appropriate.

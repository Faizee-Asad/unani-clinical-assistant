# Prompt Examples

## OPD note

```text
/unani-clinical-assistant
Mode: OPD note
Role: BUMS intern
Case: Adult patient, 3 months constipation, bloating, poor sleep, sedentary work, no blood in stool reported. Vitals not available. Need OPD note, missing questions, red flags, and patient education. Do not prescribe.
```

## Viva prep

```text
/unani-clinical-assistant
Mode: viva prep
Topic: Zeequn Nafas
Need: one-page revision, Unani concept map, modern correlation, 20 viva questions, model answers, red flags.
```

## Drug monograph

```text
/unani-clinical-assistant
Mode: drug monograph
Drug: [name]
Need: structured monograph template. Do not invent dose or references. Mark all unverified fields.
```

## Patient handout

```text
/unani-clinical-assistant
Mode: patient education
Topic: sleep hygiene for a patient with poor sleep and stress
Language: simple English
Need: no medicines, warning signs, follow-up advice.
```
